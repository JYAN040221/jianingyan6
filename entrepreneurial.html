MelTown Music Overview

MelTown Music is a cultural IP rooted in the Chinese international student community in Oceania, dedicated to discovering and supporting Chinese original music and art. It promotes original music and artistic trends through diverse entertainment content, including audio-visual channels and live events across multiple music genres. MelTown Music aims to create a rich cultural brand with a sense of belonging for international students by offering a platform for artistic expression.

Live Events

The live segment focuses on delivering high-quality live music events, featuring talented musicians and hosting competitive showcases. Our events include regular live performances and concerts that bring together artists from diverse backgrounds. These events serve as a platform for emerging artists to showcase their talents.

Content Production

The content segment creates a variety of online content, such as short videos, variety shows, dramas, and podcasts, centered on the international student experience and original artistic elements. These efforts help promote the IP brand while supporting the live events through cross-platform engagement and original music promotion.

By integrating both online and offline elements, MelTown Music enriches the cultural life of the international student community with engaging content and events.

Offline Segment

Concept Evolution

MTPE 2.0 focused on hip-hop, while MTPE 3.0 covered diverse genres. This evolution demonstrates our commitment to expanding musical diversity and providing a broader platform for artistic expression.

Event Highlights

Hosted two successful live events promoting Chinese original music. Led a 20-person team, managing event planning and promotion, and collaborated with universities and venues.

Marketing Strategy

Used surveys to target prime times and partnered with bars, offering group discounts. This data-driven approach significantly enhanced our market reach and audience engagement.

Impact

Achieved a 220% increase in attendance and a profit of 9,000 AUD, demonstrating the success of our strategic planning and community-focused approach.

Online Segment

Drama "DAMN!! Melbourne" | Executive Producer

"DAMN!! Melbourne" is a sitcom that depicts the daily musical lives of five distinct young individuals sharing a house in Melbourne. Production Coordination and Budget Management: Managed the production schedule for 14 actors and a 26-member crew, and coordinated with sponsors and business partners.

The sitcom format allows us to explore diverse storylines while maintaining a consistent focus on music and youth culture. Each episode features original music performances integrated naturally into the plot, showcasing the talents of our cast members.

Drama "Under the Cross Star" | Creative Director

"Under the Cross Star" is a vertical-screen short drama series, with each 15-minute episode portraying the love stories of international university students in Melbourne. Filming and Review: Filmed the videos according to the shooting schedule with two actors per episode and managed an 11-member production team. The series received 130k+ likes across all platforms.

The vertical format and focus on international student relationships resonated strongly with our target audience, leading to viral sharing and high engagement rates. The series success has opened doors for future collaborations and sponsorship opportunities.
