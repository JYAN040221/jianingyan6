The Haunted (2023)

A production by Melbourne Chinese Theatre Society that tells the story of a struggling novelist who finds healing through unexpected encounters with ghostly residents in a haunted house. As the Director Secretary, I contributed to making this unique production a success, achieving over 400 attendees across four performances.

Performance Coaching

Coordinated schedules between actors and directors, organised rehearsals, and provided advice on acting based on the overall plan. This comprehensive approach ensured consistency in performance quality and character development throughout the production.

Rehearsal Management

Successfully managed rehearsal schedules for a large team of over 95 members, ensuring smooth execution of all production elements. This involved careful coordination of various departments including acting, technical, and production teams.

Coordination

Served as the primary communication bridge between actors, directors, and production teams. Effectively conveyed directorial vision and production requirements to actors, while ensuring their needs and concerns were addressed to maintain a positive and productive working environment.

Production Impact

The play received acclaim for its innovative plot and impressive stage effects. The successful run of four performances with 400+ attendees demonstrated the production's strong appeal and the team's effective collaboration.

The Boor (2024)

As the Executive Director for Melbourne Chinese Theatre Society's musical adaptation of "The Boor," I led a team of over 100 production members in creating a contemporary interpretation that explores societal absurdities and human complexity. The production successfully engaged 400+ audience members with its innovative approach to classical theater.

Script Adaptation

Led the adaptation process from play to musical, working closely with the script creation team and screenwriter. Focused on revising the script structure and dialogue to enhance stage presentation while maintaining character depth and plot coherence. This collaborative effort resulted in a compelling narrative that resonated with modern audiences.

Directorial Work

Coordinated with two directors and guided three main actors through the production process. Ensured that the artistic vision was consistently maintained while allowing for creative interpretation and character development. The intimate cast size enabled focused attention on performance nuances and character relationships.

Stage Design

Actively participated in the stage set design process, collaborating with team members on crucial elements including stage layout, lighting design, and prop selection. Supervised the installation process to ensure all technical aspects aligned with the production's artistic vision.

Production Management

Successfully managed a large production team of over 100 members, coordinating various aspects of the musical from rehearsals to technical implementation. The production's success was reflected in its strong audience turnout and positive reception.
